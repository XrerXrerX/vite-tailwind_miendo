# Security Policy

If you discover a security vulnerability in Miendo,
please report it to security@lintasinovasiglobal.com

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will respond
to security reports within 48 hours.

# Out of Scope

- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks
- Denial of service attacks
- Spam or social media attacks

Thank you for helping keep Miendo secure!
